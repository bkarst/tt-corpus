# Trivia Tango - Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## 1. Executive Summary
**Trivia Tango** is positioning itself to disrupt the mobile trivia space by combining high-stakes, turn-based quiz battles with an addictive artifact collection meta-game ("The Venue") and deep educational content ("Story Cards"). This GTM strategy outlines how we will acquire, retain, and monetize players by leveraging our unique gameplay loops and competitive skill tracking.

## 2. Target Audience & User Personas

### Persona A: The Competitive Brainiac
- **Demographics:** 18-35, highly educated, naturally competitive.
- **Motivations:** Proving their knowledge, climbing skill rankings.
- **Key Features:** Advanced Glicko Rating System, Turn-Based Duels, Category Counter-Picks.

### Persona B: The Collector / Completionist
- **Demographics:** 25-45, enjoys progression systems and idle games.
- **Motivations:** Unlocking new items, organizing collections, visual progression.
- **Key Features:** The Venue, Mystery Boxes, Artifact Maintenance, Rarity Tiers (Common to Mythical).

### Persona C: The Casual Learner
- **Demographics:** 30-55+, curious, enjoys podcasts and audiobooks.
- **Motivations:** Learning new facts, relaxed gameplay.
- **Key Features:** Deep-Dive Story Cards (with text-to-speech), Single Player "Quick Play", No time pressure.

## 3. Unique Selling Propositions (USPs)
To stand out in a crowded market, our messaging will focus on what makes Trivia Tango unique:
1. **Trivia Meets Collection:** You don't just answer questions; you build a world-class Palace of rare artifacts.
2. **Auto-Match Intelligence:** Instantly generates multiplayer matches tailored to the favorite subjects of *both* players, reducing friction.
3. **True Skill Tracking:** Move beyond basic win/loss records with Glicko-based ratings tracked by specific categories.
4. **The Learning Never Stops:** Fully-voiced, text-to-speech Story Cards reveal the hidden histories behind the questions.

## 4. Acquisition Strategy

### A. Viral & Social Mechanics
- **Deep Link Invites:** Leverage the `/invite/[token]` system. When a user invites a friend via a deep link, both the sender (50 Gems) and the claimant (25 Gems) are rewarded.
- **Shareable Results:** Highly visual end-of-game screens with confetti and head-to-head stats designed to be shared on social media (Instagram Stories, X, TikTok).

### B. Paid Acquisition (User Acquisition - UA)
- **Video Ads:** Showcase the progression of the Venue (from dusty Locker to Palace) to attract the Collector persona.
- **Playable Ads:** Mini trivia questions where answering correctly "unlocks" a Mystery Box, driving direct installs.
- **Targeting:** Target interests in existing trivia games (Trivia Crack, Jeopardy), brain training apps (Elevate, Lumosity), and collection/idle games.

### C. App Store Optimization (ASO)
- Implement our optimized `AppStore.md` copy.
- A/B test the first 3 screenshots, specifically testing "Core Gameplay" vs. "The Venue Collection" to see which drives higher conversion.

## 5. Retention & Engagement Strategy
- **Daily Missions & Streaks:** The Home dashboard tracks daily missions (e.g., "Play 3 History games") and consecutive win streaks, heavily incentivizing daily logins with Coin rewards.
- **Mystery Box Timers:** Appointment mechanics. Boxes take time to unlock (e.g., 8 hours, 12 hours), prompting users to enable push notifications and return to the app to claim their loot.
- **Artifact Maintenance:** Artifacts degrade over time and require cleaning, ensuring the Collector persona regularly engages with their Venue.

## 6. Monetization Strategy
- **Trivia Tango Pro (Subscription):** Targets highly engaged users. Offers an entirely ad-free experience plus a monthly bounty of 500 Coins and 50 Gems.
- **In-App Purchases (IAP):** Direct purchase of Gems and Coins to speed up Mystery Box timers, unlock premium categories, or buy featured artifacts in the Store.
- **Ad Monetization:** Rewarded video ads (e.g., "Watch a video to halve your Mystery Box timer" or "Watch a video for an extra hint").

## 7. Launch Roadmap

### Phase 1: Soft Launch (Geo-Restricted)
- **Target:** Tier 2/3 English-speaking markets (e.g., Canada, Australia, Philippines).
- **Goal:** Test server stability, balance the in-game economy (Coin/Gem output vs. Store prices), and establish baseline Day 1, Day 7, and Day 30 retention rates.

### Phase 2: Global Launch
- **Target:** Worldwide, focusing heavily on the US and UK.
- **Action:** Scale up Paid UA campaigns, activate influencer partnerships, and heavily promote the "Invite a Friend" Gem rewards.

### Phase 3: Post-Launch LiveOps
- Introduce limited-time "Featured Artifacts" in the Store.
- Release seasonal Mega Categories (e.g., "Spooky History" for Halloween).

## 8. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Cost Per Install (CPI):** Keep below industry averages by optimizing ad creatives.
- **Day 1 / Day 7 Retention:** Crucial for measuring the stickiness of the core trivia loop and the Mystery Box timers.
- **Average Revenue Per Daily Active User (ARPDAU):** Monitored to balance the free economy vs. IAP conversion.
- **K-Factor (Virality):** Tracking how many new users are generated via the deep-link invite system.
